The SNS Project Governance category is established to facilitate discussions and decisions related to governance surrounding specific dapps and services on the IC which have launched or are planning to launch an SNS, including SNS-related feature designs, etc.

- Why should people use this category? What is it for? 

The purpose of this category is to discuss SNS Specific Governance related topics, and projects that have or are planning to launch an SNS.

- How exactly is this different than the other categories we already have?

This is for specific discussion around the SNS projects and their governance. Other governance related sub-categories are for discussion of NNS Governance, and Non-SNS DAO's on the IC.

- What should topics in this category generally contain?

This topic should contain discussion around and specific to governance relating to SNS Projects.
