The NNS Governance category is established to facilitate discussions and decisions related to governance surrounding the IC Network Nervous System (NNS).

- Why should people use this category? What is it for? 

The purpose of this category is to discuss NNS Specific Governance related topics. IC and NNS governance related topics such as tokenomics, etc.

- How exactly is this different than the other categories we already have?

This is for specific discussion around the NNS. Other governance related sub-categories are for discussion of SNS projects, and Non-SNS DAO's on the IC.

- What should topics in this category generally contain?

This topic should contain discussion around and specific to the Network Nervous System.
