## **Project highlights**

Galactic bridge is a cross-chain bridge that allows users to move Solana to ICP and vice versa. It is based on chain-key cryptography and Solana treasury program. Learn more here: https://weichain.gitbook.io/galactic-bridge

## **Features**

- Turn Solana into gSol on ICP
- Turn gSol to Sol
- Have a permissionless token  Representing Solana on ICP

## **How to install**

Open https://galactic-bridge-tzbjl.ondigitalocean.app/.
Connect your Phantom wallet and mint gSol
