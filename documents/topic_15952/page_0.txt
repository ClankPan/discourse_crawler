## Summary

[Trail of Bits](https://www.trailofbits.com/about), is a highly-rated cybersecurity research and consulting firm which has done security audits of dozens of blockchains. In May 2022, Trail of Bits and the DFINITY Foundation worked together to audit the design and implementation of the "integration of the threshold ECDSA signature scheme with its consensus protocol and Bitcoin canister and adapter."
