Francis | 2023-11-25 14:13:55 UTC | #33

This has since changed to 1.9MB. At the time of writing the white paper the smart contract had not yet been optimised for performance. Considering the block size limit is 4MB, we decided to opt for a chunk size just below the ingress limit of 2MB. This allows room for other ingress messages with potentially higher priority to be included, and did not want to be limited to one payload per block which could lead to a slower upload speed.
